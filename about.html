Personal Details

I am a 3rd year undergraduate student of the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. I am currently a part of Finance & Operations Head at Developers' Society, IIT Kharagpur. Formerly, I worked as an Associate Manager at Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur. During my tenure, I was responsible for developing websites for the various Entrepreneurship Cell events. Simultaneously, I mentored a team of fifteen GES Interns to learn various web development technologies. I have participated in many web development hackathons and won a few of them.

I love developing things and I am also good at designing things. So I found that Web Development suits my interest and skillset both. That's why I started learning web development. Now being a part of Developers' Society at IIT Kharagpur I am trying to help the student community at IIT Kharagpur to get acquainted with the world of development.

In March 2021, I with two of my firends participated in a Website Design-a-thon organized by Dare2Compete and Uttar Pradesh Industrial Development Authority. We had to design a website for UPSIDA in a period of 15 days. We successfully finished the task and came in the top ten contesting teams of the Hackathon. Later in the month of december, I won a website hackathon organized by Technology Students' Gymkhana, IIT Kharagpur along with a team of 11 members.

In summer 2022, I successfully grabbed the offer for SDE Intern at Amazon for the summers of 2023 through our college internship drive. Currently, I am exploring the field of Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence. I am open to remote internships in the field of software development and Data Science.
